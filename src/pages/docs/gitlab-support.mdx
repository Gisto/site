## Overview

In addition to GitHub snippets, Gisto now supports GitLab Snippets, allowing you to manage code snippets from multiple platforms in one unified application. Connect your GitLab account and seamlessly integrate your existing snippets with Gisto.

## Connecting Your GitLab Account

To connect your GitLab account:

1. Open Gisto login page
2. Select "GitLab" as your provider
3. Insert GitLab token

## Working with GitLab Snippets

Once connected, your GitLab snippets will appear. You can:

- **Search and filter** snippets from both providers using the same search interface
- **Create new snippets** on GitLab directly through Gisto
- **Edit existing snippets** stored on GitLab
- **Tag and organize** your GitLab snippets the same way as other snippets
- **Share and manage** visibility settings for your GitLab snippets
