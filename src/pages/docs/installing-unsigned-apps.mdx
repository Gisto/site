## About Code Signing

This application is part of an open-source project and is currently distributed using a **self-signed certificate**. At this time, we do not use commercial code-signing or notarization services.

As a result, your operating system may display security warnings when launching the application.

> ⚠️ **Important:** These warnings are expected for unsigned or self-signed applications and do not necessarily indicate that the software is unsafe.

To ensure your safety, always download the application from the **official release page of this repository**.

---

## What to Expect

### **Windows**
You may see warnings such as:
- *"Windows protected your PC"*
- *"Unrecognized app"*

### **macOS**
You may encounter a message such as:
- *"This app is from an unidentified developer"*

---

## Installation Instructions

### **Windows**

1. When the warning dialog appears, click **More info**.
2. Click **Run anyway** to proceed.

---

### **macOS**

macOS may block the application on first launch. To open it:

**Option 1: Context Menu**
1. Right-click (or Control-click) the application.
2. Select **Open**, then confirm.

**Option 2: System Settings**
1. Open **System Settings → Privacy & Security**.
2. Scroll down and click **Open Anyway**.

---
