## Overview

Gisto now supports local snippet storage, allowing you to create and manage code snippets without requiring a connection to GitHub or GitLab. This is perfect for private, sensitive, or temporary code that you don't want to sync to external platforms.

## Creating Local Snippets

To create a local snippet:

Open Gisto and click on "Create New Snippet". The flow is similar to creating a regular snippet.

Your snippet will be stored locally on your device.

## Managing Local Snippets

Local snippets work just like cloud-synced snippets:

- **Search and filter** your local snippets using all available search options
- **Tag and organize** for better management
- **Edit and update** your snippets anytime
- **Delete** snippets when no longer needed

## Benefits of Local Storage

- **Privacy**: Keep sensitive code completely private
- **Offline Access**: Access your snippets without internet connection
- **No External Dependencies**: Use Gisto without account setup
- **Flexibility**: Mix local and cloud-stored snippets as needed

Local snippets are stored securely on your device and give you complete control over your code.
You can import and export snippets between devices via the import/export section in the setting.
